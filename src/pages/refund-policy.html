Refund Policy

All sales through KV Garage are final unless otherwise stated in writing. Due to the nature of wholesale, private supply, and custom orders, refunds are not guaranteed once production or sourcing has begun.

Wholesale & Bulk Orders

Wholesale and bulk orders are considered final once payment is received and production or allocation has started. No refunds will be issued for delays caused by manufacturers, logistics providers, or customs.

Custom & Branded Orders

Custom or branded orders (including logo work, private labeling, or special specifications) are non-refundable once approved and submitted to production.

Damaged or Incorrect Orders

If an order arrives damaged or incorrect, you must notify us within 48 hours of delivery with clear documentation. Approved issues may qualify for replacement or credit at our discretion.

Contact

For questions regarding refunds or order issues, please contact us directly through the contact page.
